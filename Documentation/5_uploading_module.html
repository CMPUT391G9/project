5. Uploading Module

This module will be used by data curators to upload images, audio recordings, and scalar measurements.

5.1 Enter a Audio Record

Clicking the "Upload Records" hyperlink will directe the data curators to the record uploading page.

First, choose the audio record which record the data from the Scientists who record the data.

Then, enter the following information:

Click the "UPLOAD" button to upload the record, and the data curators will be directed to images uploading page.

5.2 Upload Images

In the images uploading page, click the "Browse" button to choose one or more images stored in the data curator's local file system.

Afterimages are chosen, click the "UploadPic" button to upload the images to the system database.

5.1 Upload Scalar Measurements

Clicking the "Upload Measurements" hyperlink will directe the data curators to the upload measurements page.

Click the "UPLOAD" button to upload the record, and the data curators will be directed to images uploading page.
